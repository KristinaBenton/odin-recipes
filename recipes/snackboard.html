Snack Board

Recipe Description

This is my own vegetarian take on an "adult lunchable".

Snack boards are incredibly versitile as far as ingredients go.

I pretend I'm in a tv drama every single time I make one. Pairs well with a glass of wine..

Ingredients

  • 1/2c mixed nuts
  • 1/2 block of sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 block of smoked gouda cheese
  • 1/4c dried crandberries
  • 1c Everything Crackers
  • 1/2c grapes
  • half an english cucumber
  • 16oz of whole wheat crustinis
  • 1/4c of fig butter

Steps

  1. Rinse and chop cucumber.
  2. Slice cheese into bite-sized pieces
  3. Assemble all ingredients onto a serving plate or clean cutting board
  4. Pour yourself a glass of wine and enjoy with an episode of Bridgerton or The Crown.
