Cucumber Salad

Recipe Description

This is a homemade recipe that I came up with where I combine most of my favorite vegetables and a can of beans.

The dressing is a basic red wine vinaigrette that can easily be tweaked to taste.

This recipe is easily adaptable to whatever vegetables one might have in their fridge.

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1 English Cucumber
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 handful chopped parsley or cilantro
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 can of Cannellini Beans, drained and rinsed

Vinaigrette

  • 1Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1/2tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2tsp oregano
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/2tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Rinse and chop all vegetables.
  2. Combine vegetables in large bowl
  3. Mix all Vinaigrette ingredients together in a smaller bowl and stir.
  4. Pour dressing over salad and mix to distribute.
  5. Enjoy immediately or let chill in refrigerator first.
