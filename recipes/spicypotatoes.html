Spicy Potatoes

Recipe Description

This is a homemade recipe that my fiance and I came up with that is somewhat of a copycat for Taco Bell's Spicy Potatoes.

Through trial and error, we discovered that peeling the potatoes and then air frying them created the perfect crispy outside with soft inside.

We primarily use them in breakfast burritos, but they also make a great side to just about any dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 Russet Potatoes
  • 1Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2tsp chili powder
  • 1/2tsp paprika]
  • 1/2tsp cumin
  • 1/2tsp oregano
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/2tsp pepper
  • 1/4tsp onion powder
  • 1/4tsp cayenne

Steps

  1. Peel and cube potatoes.
  2. Put cubed potatoes in a bowl and add olive oil.
  3. Add all spices to bowl and mix to evenly distribute.
  4. Pour seasoned potatoes into air fryer and cook on 400deg for 20min. Shake halfway through.
  5. Enjoy!
